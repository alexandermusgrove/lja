LJA today

Employee-owned, multi-disciplinary consulting engineering with expanding capabilities, stronger regional reach, and growing acquisition-driven complexity.

The challenge

  • Knowledge is fragmented across business units, regions, acquired firms, and core systems.
  • Utilization remains below the 70% utilization target. Engineers and PMC lose billable hours to search, re-creating prior work, tracking project context, and finding the right experts.
  • Limited access to prior work and internal expertise constrains growth and profitability across offices and projects. Newly acquired firms may sub out work that could be done in-house.
  • Proposal work slows when teams have to hunt for project history, precedent, and the right SMEs.
  • Each acquisition adds value to the business, but compounds the knowledge challenge. LJA’s collective expertise keeps growing while the ability to use it across BUs slows.

What Glean is

A governed AI system of context for a decentralized engineering firm.

Built for enterprises with many systems, strict security requirements, and internal processes that don’t live in one clean workflow.

AI system of context

Glean connects LJA’s people, content, and systems so teams can find reliable answers faster across a fragmented environment.

Search, assistant, and agents

One place to search, ask questions, and run task-specific workflows grounded in enterprise context.

Open and governed

Glean helps LJA unlock value across Microsoft systems, file servers, CRM, ERP, legacy systems, and engineering knowledge sources without requiring cleanup projects or mass migrations.

Ambitious outcomes tied to revenue, margin, and acquisition value.
